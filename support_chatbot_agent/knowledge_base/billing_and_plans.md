# Billing and Plans

Acme Cloud offers several subscription plans billed per seat, per month.

## Plans

- **Free**: 1 seat, limited usage, community support.
- **Starter**: 49 USD per seat per month, includes email support.
- **Growth**: 199 USD per seat per month, includes priority support and SLAs.
- **Scale**: 599 USD per seat per month, includes a dedicated account manager.

## Managing your subscription

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Settings -> Billing.
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of the
billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Payment methods

We accept all major credit cards and process payments securely through Stripe.
To update your card, go to Settings -> Billing -> Payment method. Invoices are
emailed automatically and are also available for download in the billing portal.

## Refund policy

Our refund policy offers a 14-day money-back guarantee for first-time
subscribers. To request a refund, contact support within 14 days of your initial
purchase. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 10
business days.

## Cancelling

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings -> Billing -> Cancel subscription.
Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, after
which your account reverts to the Free plan. Your data is retained for 30 days.
