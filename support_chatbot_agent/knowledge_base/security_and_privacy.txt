Security and Privacy

Data encryption
All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher, and encrypted at rest
using AES-256. Encryption keys are managed by our cloud provider's key
management service and rotated regularly.

Two-factor authentication
We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts.
You can enable 2FA from Settings -> Security. We support authenticator apps such
as Google Authenticator and Authy, as well as hardware security keys.

Data residency
Enterprise customers on the Scale plan can choose the region where their data is
stored, including the United States and the European Union, to meet data
residency requirements.

Compliance
Acme Cloud is SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR compliant. A copy of our SOC 2
report is available to Growth and Scale customers under a non-disclosure
agreement. Contact your account manager to request it.

Deleting your data
You can permanently delete your account and associated data from Settings ->
Account -> Delete account. Deletion is irreversible and completes within 30
days across all backups.
