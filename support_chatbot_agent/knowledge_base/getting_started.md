# Getting Started with Acme Cloud

Welcome to Acme Cloud, the platform for hosting and scaling your applications.

## Creating an account

To create an account, visit https://app.acme.example and click "Sign up". You
can register with an email address and password, or use Google / GitHub single
sign-on. A verification email is sent immediately; click the link in that email
to activate your account.

## Resetting your password

If you forgot your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot password".
Enter the email address associated with your account and we will send a password
reset link. The link expires after 60 minutes for security. If you do not
receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact
support.

## Logging in

Once your account is active, log in at https://app.acme.example with your email
and password. If you enabled two-factor authentication, you will also be asked
for a six-digit code from your authenticator app.

## Inviting teammates

Team owners can invite teammates from Settings -> Members -> Invite. Each
teammate occupies one seat on your plan. You can remove members at any time to
free up seats.
